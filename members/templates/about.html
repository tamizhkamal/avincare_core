Who We Are and What We Do?

Avin Shukri Pharmaceuticals Pvt. Ltd., headquartered in Chennai, India, is one of the fastest-growing pharmaceutical companies, specializing in the manufacturing, marketing, and distribution of high-quality, affordable medicines, cosmetics, and surgical products.

Our Comprehensive Product Portfolio:

  • Injections
  • Tablets
  • Capsules
  • Ointments
  • Creams
  • Dry Syrups
  • Oral Suspensions

We serve both generic and branded markets with a strong presence in India and strategic international markets such as Chad, South Sudan, Sudan, Egypt, and Bangladesh. We are committed to addressing global healthcare needs across two continents.

Backed by a focus on quality, regulatory compliance, and customer trust, Avin Shukri is driven by a vision to deliver reliable healthcare solutions with consistency and integrity.
